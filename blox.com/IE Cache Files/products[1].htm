The presentation application uses Blox.com's simple interface and pull down menus, making it extremely easy to create and publish interactive Web content. Slide shows can be built and then universally viewed by millions of people. Presentations can be used to deliver proposals, training, sales pitches, demos, earnings announcements, portfolios, and business plans. Better yet, you can build an entire mini Web site with the Blox.com Presentation tool. Instantly distribute Web publishing to non-programmers by letting them create and serve the content through their own personal documents and file directory. Users have access to their presentation files from virtually any Web-connected computer. Professionals of any kind can create, access and update their presentations from anywhere, and can deliver information universally through the click of a URL. No more emailing bloated attachments or forcing people to download plug-ins. The presentation application has an innovative, familiar application interface that makes content publishing via the Web as easy as using your everyday applications.

Presentation features include: outline view, slide sorter and slideshow views, multiple master slides, background patterns, blends, picture, line and box elements. It has drag and drop editing, and direct manipulation of text in outline and slide views. You can also access an online dictionary, thesaurus, and a "cliché-finder" to speed up your work.
